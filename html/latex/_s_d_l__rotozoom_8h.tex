Rotates a 32 bit surface in increments of 90 degrees. 

Specialized 90 degree rotator which rotates a \textquotesingle{}src\textquotesingle{} surface in 90 degree increments clockwise returning a new surface. Faster than rotozoomer since not scanning or interpolation takes place. Input surface must be 32 bit. (code contributed by J. Schiller, improved by C. Allport and A. Schiffler)
